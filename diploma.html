MECHANICAL ENGINEERING (DIPLOMA)

Mechanical Engineering is one of the oldest core branches of Engineering. This branch of engineering deals with design, manufacturing and operation of machinery. It uses concepts of engineering, physics, and material science for design, manufacturing, operation and maintenance of mechanical system. This branch of engineering touches our day to day lives directly or indirectly. From household equipments like washing machine and grinders to buses and trains that we use for transportation, mechanical engineering and machines are involved in one way or the other.

With the development in technology, new topics have also been introduced, like-CADD (Computer Aided Design and Drawing), nanotechnology, mechatronics etc.

ELECTRICAL ENGINEERING (DIPLOMA)

Electrical Engineering is one of the core branches of engineering. That is why it is one of the evergreen branches. Qualified electrical engineers are needed by most manufacturing and production industries to take care of tasks like power transmission, power usage, power auditing, machine monitoring, machine maintenance etc.

Electrical engineers are recruited by construction companies to take care of wiring as well as electrical works. Government job opportunities are also available in front of electrical engineers. Central and State Government setups like Indian Railways, State Electricity Boards, ONGC, DRDO, Indian Armed Forces, PWD; Muncipal Corporations are recruiting electrical engineers in larger number.
