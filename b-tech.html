B-Tech

MECHANICAL ENGINEERING

Mechanical Engineering students require a concrete understanding of key concepts. The B. Tech in Mechanical engineering is a comprehensive degree program. The broad and flexible program allows students to meet their objectives and particular career goals in a wide spectrum of industries.

CIVIL ENGINEERING

The B.Tech program of the Civil Engineering imparts students with mastery in the field of Civil Engineering besides fluency in Mathematics, Physics, Instrumentation, Computes, Management and economics which are quite essential for any successful Civil Engineer. Students gain practical experience from field visits conducted by the department to industries, Dams and Construction sites etc.

Students are not only made experts in technical aspects but also in interpersonal skills which is highly required to excel in the fast- paced world.

The department also runs M.Tech program in “Structural Engineering” since 2014-15 academic sessions.

ELECTRICAL ENGINEERING

The faculty in the department is a rich blend of personnel with industrial and professional experience. The department encourages student to undergo industrial training at various organizations for in-depth, practical exposure. The college is fully equipped with Electrical Engineering lab , Network Devices Lab, Electrical Machines Labs, Energy Conversion Devices Lab , Electrical Measurement Lab , Control & Instrumentation Lab , Power Electronics Lab , Electrical Drives Lab , Simulations Lab , Power Systems Lab , Project Lab as per the university syllabus.

ELECTRICAL AND ELECTRONICS ENGINEERING

Electrical and Electronics Engineering is a continuously evolving subject. Due to the advancement of technology the modern Engineers are facing a lot of challenges. This branch of study in collaboration with other disciplines opens new engineering avenues. The curriculum is designed in such a way to create professional Electrical & Electronics Engineers. They can meet the demands in the fields of core Electrical Engineering and other related fields.

COMPUTER SCIENCE ENGINEERING

The mission of the department is to advance, evolve and enhance Computer Science and software Engineering fundamentals to build the future of computing and IT systems of the globe. The B. Tech in CSE is a broad and flexible degree engineering program which is designed to reflect the depth and breadth of Compute Science.

ELECTRONICS AND COMMUNICATION ENGINEERING

Electronics & Communication Engineering is a rapidly changing emerging field in which the new ideas are coming up in every other second. The flexible program of B. Tech. in ECE prepares the students for careers in this constantly evolving discipline. Students can prepare for technological careers in Industry, Academia or Research.
