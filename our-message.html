Our Message


Dr. Madhumita Parida
Chairperson

Education is a process of imparting knowledge, developing the power of reasoning, judgement, wisdom, special skills, and intellectual enrichment for a mature life.

AIET stands as a trend setter for knowledge enhancement programmes where upcoming professions have a proper admixture of both theoretical and practical knowledge before entering into the actual technical and managerial career. The institute offers a unique synchronization of the best in contemporary technical education and value based personal enrichment programmes that enable the young scholars to live a worthy life through application of intellectual riches and ethical prowess. Education poses special challenges in today's highly technological and competitive world. With deep sense of self confidence and true belief of value education I am glad that the new comers will out sign in academics and prove themselves as competent professionals and leaders managing change and transformation in globally competitive market. It has always been our conscious, concerted and coordinated effort to nurture global learners by providing wholesome learning opportunities and student centered approach incorporating both the traditional and modern values.



Prof. Dr. Sudhansu Sekhar Khuntia
Principal

Our vision of Aryan Institute of Engineering & Technology is to develop technology orientation & analytical capabilities of every students.

The Institute is committed to provide the students, broad, best fundamental Engineering education & prepare the student for carrier in industry,teaching research and other organization. we also plan to develop entrepreneurial skill in students so that they would drive the sprit of growth of our economy and would be able to generate employment opportunity for other qualified skilled people.
