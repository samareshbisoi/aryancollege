Why Through JEE (Main) Or OJEE ?

Joint Entrance Examination (JEE) Main is an all India common engineering entrance examination of objective pattern which is conducted for admission into various engineering colleges and courses all over the country. Odisha Joint Entrance Examination (OJEE) is a state-government controlled centralized test for admission to all the private and governmental engineering institutions in Odisha. So for admission into 1st year B.Tech , a student has to appear for Joint Entrance Examination(JEE Main) conducted by CBSE or Odisha Joint Entrance Examination(OJEE).

What About Admission Process And Eligibility For Admission ?

B.Tech (Regular) : 4 years
For admission into 1st year B.Tech programme a student has to appear for JOINT ENTRANCE EXAMINATION (JEE MAIN) conducted by CBSE. For details please visit www.jeemain.nic.in . After successful completion of +2 Science with Mathematics, Physics & Chemistry/Biology/Biotechnology/ Computer Science with a minimum of 45% of aggregate marks (40% in case of candidates belonging to SC/ST category) in Physics, Mathematics & Chemistry /Biology/Biotechnology/Computer Science is a must.

B.Tech Lateral Entry (B.Sc): 3 years
Passed Bachelor’s Degree Examination of three years duration in Science from University of Odisha or from a recognized University as defined by UGC, with at least 45% marks (40% in case of candidates belonging to SC/ST category) and must have passed XII standard with Mathematics as a subject.

B.Tech Lateral Entry (Diploma Engineering): 3 Years
Passed three years diploma examination (two years in case of Lateral entry Diploma) in Engineering /Technology from State Council for Technical Education and Vocational Training (SCTE&VT). Lower age 14 Years and no bar for upper age.

M.Tech
For admission to first year Master Degree programme in Engineering/Technology: GATE qualified candidates or passed B.Tech/B.E. examination from a recognized university of Odisha or from an AICTE approved Institute or from a recognized University as defined by UGC with at least 50% marks (45% in case of candidates belonging to SC/ST category) at the qualifying examination.

Diploma Engineering Admission:
Admission to 1st Semester Diploma in Engineering Pass in HSC examination conducted by BSE,Odisha/10th standard examination declared equivalent by BSE ,Odisha and obtained at least 35% marks in aggregate ,securing 30% marks in each subject at the qualifying examination with English,Math & Science subjects.

Admission to 3rd Semester Diploma in Engineering (Lateral):
Pass in +2 Science Examination from CHSE, ODISHA or its equivalent examination with PCM/+2 vocational (2 years course) in any Engineering Trades/2 years ITI in Engineering Trade/COE Trade with pass in HSC examination conducted by BSE Odisha /10th standard examination declared equivalent by BSE,Odisha securing 30% marks in each subject at the qualifying examination.
