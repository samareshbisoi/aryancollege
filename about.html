About Aryan Institute of Engineering and Technology

WELCOME TO ARYAN

Just 10 kms away from Bhubaneswar International Airport, situated in a lush-green campus alongside the picturesque hillock Labanagiri near village Barakuda , Aryan Institute of Engineering & Technology came to being in 2009 & has since then been imparting B.Tech , M.Tech & Diploma courses in engineering and in the process has become an epicenter of technical value addition through education ,training, research & consultancy.

Within a short span of operation the institute has thus become a front runner in terms of admission intake figures-which bears ample testimony to its quality and standards in performance parameters.

This in turn, speaks volumes about the institutional focus on blending excellence, human dignity and societal as well as national interest.


Mission & Vision

OUR MISSION
To impart contemporary technical education and skills to students of different socio-economic backgrounds. To equip students with analytical learning and real life problem solving.

To make learning a continuous endeavour compatible with market needs.

To promote the spirit of leadership, entrepreneurship, innovation and ethics.

OUR VISION
To become a leading engineering institution of the state by imparting quality technical education at affordable costs to create skilled and motivated Engineers to serve the technological requirements of society in different ways.


Our Stream

BACHELOR'S DEGREE
B.Tech introduces students to core subjective knowledge that helps prepare strong foundation for budding minds.

MASTER'S DEGREE
A Masters of Technology gives knowledge specific to a particular technology which guarantees detailed knowledge and complete information in that field.

DIPLOMA
The Diploma in Engineering or Diploma in Technical Studies are programmes focused on practical and skills-oriented training.
